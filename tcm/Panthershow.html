PANTHER

"Beauty isn’t about appearances; it’s about confidence and strength."

Ann Takamaki, known as Panther, is a popular student who faces isolation due to her foreign heritage and beauty. Beneath her cheerful facade lies deep pain from witnessing her best friend, Shiho, suffer under Kamoshida’s abuse. Ann’s courage shines when she stands up against injustice, proving her strength is not just physical but emotional.

Awakening her Persona, Carmen, Ann embraces her inner fire and refuses to be objectified or controlled. As Panther, she wields her charm and fiery resolve to inspire others and fight for justice. Her journey is one of self-empowerment, teaching her to value her individuality and stand tall despite societal judgment.
