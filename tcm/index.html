Persona 5 is a JRPG developed by Atlus, released in 2016. It was one of the most successful games made by the company. In the game, you take control of a Tokyo student with the codename "Joker" The teenager was framed for assault by a corrupt politician and is now on probation, but after entering the Shujin academy Joker discovered being capable of summoning personas and form a group with other students becoming vigilantes in the city, naming themselves the "Phantom Thieves"
