QUEEN

"True justice isn’t about following rules; it’s about protecting the innocent."

Makoto Niijima, the eldest daughter of a police officer, lives under the shadow of strict expectations. As the student council president at Shujin Academy, she strives to uphold justice but struggles to find her own voice. Makoto’s transformation begins when she decides to defy the oppressive system that stifles her individuality.

Makoto’s Persona, Johanna, awakens as she decides to forge her own path and fight back against the corruption around her. As Queen, she becomes the team’s strategist and voice of reason, using her intellect and determination to guide the Phantom Thieves. Her journey symbolizes the power of self-discovery and breaking free from expectations.
