NOIR

"Strength comes from planting seeds of hope."

Haru Okumura, nicknamed Noir, is the sheltered daughter of a corrupt businessman. Polite and soft-spoken, she hides a sharp mind and a strong will. Haru’s journey begins when she rebels against her father’s oppressive control, determined to find freedom and purpose in life.

Haru’s Persona, Milady, awakens as she breaks free from her father’s expectations. As Noir, she wields grace and strength in equal measure, balancing her compassion with a fierce resolve. Her journey highlights the importance of self-reliance and standing up for one’s dreams.
