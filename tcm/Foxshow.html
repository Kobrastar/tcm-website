FOX

"Even the ugliest of truths can inspire the most beautiful art."

Yusuke Kitagawa, a gifted artist studying under the renowned painter Madarame, is a man of deep passion and eccentricity. Though initially perceived as peculiar, his dedication to beauty and art is unparalleled. Orphaned at a young age, Yusuke was taken in by Madarame, who he revered as a mentor and father figure. However, beneath his calm exterior lies a longing for genuine connection and a struggle to reconcile his artistic ideals with the harsh realities of the world. Yusuke's life begins to change when he encounters the Phantom Thieves, who uncover the truth about Madarame’s exploitation of his students and their artwork.

After confronting the betrayal by his mentor, Yusuke awakens to his Persona, Goemon, a flamboyant and legendary thief. He joins the Phantom Thieves as Fox, combining his artistic brilliance with tactical precision. His creativity extends beyond battle strategies to his unique perspective on the world, often offering profound insights—or unintentional comedic relief. Through his journey with the Thieves, Yusuke not only hones his skills as an artist but also discovers the power of camaraderie and the importance of staying true to oneself. His vibrant individuality and unwavering pursuit of beauty make him an unforgettable member of the team.
